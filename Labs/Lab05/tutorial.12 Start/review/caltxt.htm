This Year at the CCC

The upcoming year is shaping up to be our best season ever at the CCC. The schedule includes Broadway touring productions from Wicked to The Book of Mormon to an award-winning revival of West Side Story. Get in line now for great singers and musicians including Melissa Levens, Kate Larson, and Ian Watts.

The CCC continues its family-friendly lineup of classic shows. In March, we're happy to welcome back the Keystone Circus for three nights of acrobatics and fun in a single ring circus venue. Popular children's entertainer Carlyle will join us in April for an evening of his off-beat songs and stories.

As always, parking is free and available and the downtown cuisine is as delicious, varied, and reasonably priced as ever. Discount tickets are available for early reservations.
