Car Seat Calculator

This web app determines the reccomended car seat or booster needed by your child.

Please note that this is not legal advice, you should consult the laws of your state regulating child safty seating in vehicles.

Here are the rules being used to determine the child safty seating reccomendation.
Children who are:

  • At least 8 years of age, or taller than 4', 9" (57 inches), don't need a special seat.
  • 4',9" or less, must use a booster seat or car seat.
  • Less than 40 lbs., must use a car seat.
    • Age 2 or more, and weigh more than 20 lbs., may use a front-facing car seat.
    • Weigh less than 20 lbs., must use a rear-facing car seat.

These recommendations are based on infromation from Safe Ride 4 Kids
