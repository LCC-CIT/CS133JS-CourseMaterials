Hitori

To Play

Click the Puzzle 1 through Puzzle 3 buttons to select a puzzle. Click the cells in the table, switching the cell type from unknown (gray) to circled to block. A completed puzzle should have only circles and blocks.

Stumped? Click the Peek! button to highlight your errors. Incorrect numbers will show up in red. Give up? Click the Show Solution button.

About Hitori

Hitori is a Japanese logic puzzle based on a grid of numbers. Each number is either circled or blocked. The rules are:

  1. Blocks cannot be horizontally or vertically adjacent.
  2. The same number cannot appear circled more than once in any row or column.
  3. Circles must form a single path connected horizontally and vertically, but not diagonally.

Tips

  1. Once you have found a block, immediately circle the adjacent numbers in the horizontal and vertical direction.
  2. Once a number is circled, duplicate numbers in that row or column must be blocks.
  3. Any cell between duplicate numbers (horizontally or vertically) is always circled.
  4. No circled number or group of circled numbers can be entirely surrounded by blocks.
